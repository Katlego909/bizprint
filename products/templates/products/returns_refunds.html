Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our priority. Here's our returns and refunds policy.

We stand behind the quality of our work. If there's an issue with your order that's our fault, we'll make it right - either through a reprint or refund.

How to Request a Return or Refund

1

Contact Us Within 7 Days

Report the issue within 7 days of receiving your order. Email us at support@bizprint.co.za with:

  • Your order number
  • Photos of the issue
  • Description of the problem
2

Our team will review your claim within 1-2 business days and determine if it qualifies for a return or refund.

3

Resolution

If approved, we'll offer one of the following:

  • Full reprint at no charge

If we require the items returned, we'll provide a prepaid shipping label. Do not send items back without our authorization.

Damaged in Shipping

  2. Do not discard the packaging
  3. Report the damage within 24 hours of delivery
  4. We'll file a claim with the courier and arrange a replacement

Common Questions

What if I don't like how the colors turned out?

Minor color variations between screen and print are normal due to different color profiles (RGB vs CMYK). However, if colors are significantly off from what was approved in the proof, we'll investigate and reprint if it's our error.

Can I return part of my order?

Yes, if only some items in your order have issues, we can arrange a partial return and reprint just those items.

What if I made a mistake in my artwork?

Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns for errors in customer-supplied files. We recommend carefully reviewing your files before submission and requesting a proof for important orders.

How do I avoid issues with my order?

  • Use high-resolution images (300 DPI)
  • Proofread all text carefully
  • Order a proof for color-critical projects
  • Use our design service if unsure about file preparation
  • Contact support if you have questions before ordering
