Frequently Asked Questions

We offer quantity-based pricing—the more you order, the lower the unit cost. Each product shows pricing tiers (e.g., 100, 500, 1000 units). You can also add optional services like rush delivery (+50%) or professional design assistance. All prices include 15% VAT, and you'll see the complete breakdown before checkout.

Production takes 3-5 business days after payment verification and file approval. Shipping adds 3-5 days (standard R120) or 1-2 days (express R250). Need it faster? Choose Rush Production (1-2 days, +50% fee). Free collection available from our Johannesburg office. Turnaround starts only after we verify your payment and approve your files.

Absolutely! We offer three professional design packages: Basic (2 revision rounds, 3-5 days), Standard (3 revisions, 3-5 days), and Premium (unlimited revisions, priority service). You'll complete a detailed design brief, receive initial concepts, and can request revisions. Rush design service (1-2 days) is available for urgent projects. You own full rights to the final approved design.

After placing your order, you'll receive a unique reference number. Use our Track Order page to check status, upload artwork if needed, and submit proof of payment. You'll receive email updates when payment is verified, production starts, and when your order ships. You can track using either the full reference number or just the first 8 characters for convenience.

We accept PDF (preferred), PNG, and JPG files up to 5MB. Files must be 300 DPI at actual print size for best results. Use CMYK color mode for accurate printing (RGB files will be converted). Include 3mm bleed on all sides to prevent white edges. Don't have proper files? Our design service can prepare print-ready artwork for you.

We perform quality checks before shipping every order. If items arrive damaged (report within 24 hours with photos) or have printing errors on our end (report within 7 days), we'll reprint at no cost. However, we cannot accept returns for errors in customer-supplied files. We recommend requesting a digital proof for important projects and ordering small test runs for large quantities.
