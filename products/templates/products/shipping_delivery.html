Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

Yes, but only before the order is dispatched. Contact us immediately if you need to change the address.

What happens if I'm not home for delivery?

The courier will leave a notice and attempt redelivery the next business day. You can also arrange collection from the courier depot.

Do you deliver on weekends?

Standard courier services operate Monday-Friday. Weekend delivery may be available for express orders at an additional cost - contact us to arrange.

My order hasn't arrived. What should I do?

First, check your tracking information. If the delivery is delayed beyond the estimated date, contact us at support@bizprint.co.za with your order number.
